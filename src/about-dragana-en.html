Dr. Dragana (Tekić) Novaković

Dr. Dragana (Tekić) Novaković is currently employed at the Faculty of Agriculture, University of Novi Sad, where she teaches courses in Statistics, Statistical Methods, Econometrics, Biometrics, and Biostatistics at undergraduate, master's, and doctoral levels. She completed her bachelor's, master's, and doctoral studies, academic program: Agroeconomics, at the Faculty of Agriculture in Novi Sad. Her doctoral dissertation titled "Estimation Of Profitability Factors Of Agricultural And Food Companies In The Republic Of Serbia Using Panel Regression Analysis" was successfully defended in 2022.

In her scientific and research work, Dragana (Tekić) Novaković has authored or co-authored more than 50 scientific papers in journals from various fields and has participated in numerous domestic and international conferences. She has also been involved in a large number of domestic and international projects, where, in line with the objectives of the projects and her previous experience, she was responsible for statistical data processing, creating and analyzing data from questionnaires, as well as financial data analysis.

She possesses advanced knowledge related to data analysis, relying on various software for statistical data processing (R, Python, SPSS, Stata, Eviews, Statistica).
