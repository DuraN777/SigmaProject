Sigma Science

Statistical support for scientific research

The data processing services in scientific research that we offer are tailored to the needs of the research. From basic data analysis to complex models and simulations, our ability to provide accurate and relevant results enables the making of scientifically grounded conclusions. Through a transparent approach, we help you expand your research horizons and make significant scientific contributions.

Research Methodology

  • Creating the research design;
  • Sample optimization;

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost of statistical data processing?

The price of statistical data processing is determined after reviewing the data and the research design. The cost is based on the complexity and number of statistical analyses required, whether data entry is needed, the intricacy of data organization and transformation, the scope of work, and the deadlines involved.

What software do you use for data processing?

We rely on a variety of software tools for statistical data processing, including R, Python, SPSS, Stata, Eviews, and Statistica. We successfully combine these with the necessary expertise in creating and managing databases using SQL.

What is the turnaround time for completing a project?

We strive to meet your needs and deliver results as quickly as possible. However, it is important to be realistic in your expectations and understand that some projects may require 7, 10, or more days of data processing. Therefore, please contact us as early as possible.
